TeamWorking by TechNexus is a private, tech-focused workspace community located in Chicago's Civic Opera Building at 20 N. Wacker Drive. Established in 2007 as part of TechNexus Venture Collaborative, TeamWorking offers over 60,000 square feet of flexible office suites, private offices, coworking spaces, meeting rooms, and event venues for technology companies and startups. The community currently houses 75+ technology companies and has grown to more than 750 alumni, including graduates like Capital One, Rally Health, Threekit, and Ocient. Amenities include 400Mbps WiFi, a 10,000 sq ft rooftop terrace, fitness center, Shure-equipped podcast studio, and event space for up to 175 guests.
